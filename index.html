TwitchBot has tons of features for Twitch users, streamers, and gamers that will make any server more exciting.

Stay up-to-date in the world of streaming

Easily access Twitch from Discord

View details and stats from your favorite streamers on Twitch. Get current view and stream status, current game, and a preview of the stream.

See what's trending

See the most popular games and streams on Twitch with just one command. You'll also see the amount of channels streaming and total current views for the game.

Get the top highlights and clips

Search Twitch's wide selection of clips recorded by tons of users. Search by trending, a certain game, or a Twitch streamer.

Powerful streamer tools for your server

Get notified when channels go live

Get an in-chat notification when your favorite streamers go live with a single command. Just specify the channel you want the notification in and the streamer you want.

Give live users in your server something extra

Configure TwitchBot to give users who are streaming on Twitch a configurable role to show others their special status and interest them in their stream.

Even more features included

Listen in voice chat

You can listen to any live Twitch stream in voice chat with all of your friends with nearly seamless audio quality.

View player stats from your favorite games

Take a look at any player's Fortnite or Overwatch stats by entering their in-game name. See a wide variety of stats for each game.

Keep your Discord friendly and safe

Moderate your server and keep toxic users at bay with fully-featured moderation. Kick, ban, and more. You can can even add a toxicity filter to your servers so that toxic messages are automatically deleted.
